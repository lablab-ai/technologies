# AssemblyAI

AssemblyAI empowers developers with access to cutting-edge AI models for superhuman speech recognition, automatic transcription, speech summarization, and more.
With a mission to democratize accessibility of these advanced AI models, allowing developers and product teams to integrate them seamlessly into their projects on a massive scale, all through a user-friendly API.
